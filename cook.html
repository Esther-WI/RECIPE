MY RECIPE

My Ingredients

  1. Fried sausages
  2. Blended tomatoes
  3. Finely chopped onions
  4. mashed garlic ginger paste
  5. Thinly chopped green,red,yellow pepper
  6. Spices:paprika.spice and wet dry fry,cumin
  7. Coriander leaves

LETS GET
COOKING!

My Procedure

  • Slice the sausages into small pieces.
  • Fry the onions under low heat
  • Add minced garlic-ginger mixture to the pan and stir.
  • Add spoonful of all the spices to the pan and stir.
  • Put the blended tomato to the pan and stir.
  • Add the thinly chopped peppers to the pan and continue stirring.
  • Add the sliced sausages to the pan and continue stirring under low heat
  • Top it of ith some coriander leaves
  • Serve it with any food of your choice
